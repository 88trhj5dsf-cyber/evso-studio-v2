Common questions

Good to know

How do we get started? +

Fill in the contact form or drop us an email at hello@evso.studio. We'll schedule a 30-minute discovery call to understand your project, share our process, and determine if we're the right fit for each other.

Do you work with startups or only established brands? +

Both. What matters to us isn't the size of your company — it's the seriousness of your intent. We've built brands for seed-stage startups and global enterprises alike. We look for clients who care deeply about quality and understand that great design is an investment.

What does the payment structure look like? +

We work on a 50/50 model: 50% at project kickoff, 50% on final delivery before files are released. For larger or longer engagements we can discuss milestone-based payment schedules.

Do you work remotely or in-person? +

Primarily remote. We have clients across Europe, the US, and Asia. We run discovery calls and presentations over video, and use shared project management tools to keep everything transparent and collaborative. If you're in the Netherlands, we're happy to meet in person.

Can you help us after the project is delivered? +

Yes. Many of our clients return for ongoing retainer engagements — monthly design support, campaign work, or website updates. We also offer brand stewardship packages for businesses that want a long-term creative partner rather than a one-off vendor.
