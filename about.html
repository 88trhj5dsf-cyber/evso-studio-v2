EVSO Studio is a small independent practice built on the belief that the best design is invisible — it works so well you simply feel it.

Who we are

A studio built
on intention

Founded in 2019 in Haarlem, EVSO Studio grew from a shared conviction that creative work should be deliberate, honest, and beautiful. We believe the most powerful design doesn't shout — it invites.

We work with a carefully selected roster of clients — primarily European brands in architecture, wellness, hospitality, and technology — who share our appetite for restraint and long-term thinking.

Our approach
1
Less, but better
We favour reduction over decoration. Every element earns its place.
2
Collaborative by nature
We work alongside clients, not simply for them. Your insight shapes our output.
3
Built for longevity
We design systems, not just moments. Work that endures beyond the trend cycle.

What we stand for

Our values

01

Intentional simplicity

We believe complexity is the enemy of communication. We strip each project back to its essential truth and build from there — never adding for its own sake.

02

Deep listening

Before we make anything, we understand everything. Great design is a precise response to a real problem, not a solution searching for one.

03

Enduring craft

We build things to last. Every typeface choice, every proportion, every interaction is considered through the lens of time — not trend.

04

Honest partnership

We say what we think, even when it's not what you hoped to hear. Our best clients are the ones who value directness over flattery.
